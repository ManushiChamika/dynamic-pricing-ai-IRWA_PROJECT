## üö® RED FLAGS BY TOPIC

### Red Flags: Communication Protocols
**DON'T say:**
- "REST would work fine"
- "MCP is just another protocol"
- "We chose MCP arbitrarily"

**DO say:**
- "MCP gives 10-50ms vs REST's 100-200ms"
- "MCP is industry standard for tool-using agents"
- "MCP has built-in auditability"

**Find:** COMMUNICATION_QUICK_REFERENCE.md, VIVA_QUICK_REFERENCE.md

---

### Red Flags: Fairness Checks
**DON'T say:**
- "Fairness is checked after approval"
- "Fairness is just a warning"
- "We can override fairness rules"

**DO say:**
- "Fairness is architectural enforcement in Alert Service"
- "Proposals are rejected if fairness fails"
- "3 checks: variance, margin, undercut"

**Find:** VIVA_QUICK_REFERENCE.md, AGENT_COMMUNICATION_GUIDE.md

---

### Red Flags: Audit Trail
**DON'T say:**
- "events.jsonl is just debug logs"
- "Events are optional"
- "We can delete events"

**DO say:**
- "events.jsonl is immutable audit trail with 182K+ events"
- "Used for compliance, debugging, and learning"
- "Write-once, read-many design"

**Find:** VIVA_QUICK_REFERENCE.md, AGENT_COMMUNICATION_GUIDE.md

---

### Red Flags: Responsible AI
**DON'T say:**
- "Responsible AI is nice-to-have"
- "Fairness is subjective"
- "Black-box algorithms are fine"

**DO say:**
- "Responsible AI is architectural (fairness enforcement + auditability + transparency)"
- "Fairness has measurable thresholds (5%, 10%, 2x)"
- "Every decision is logged and explainable"

**Find:** COMPLETE_PACKAGE_SUMMARY.md, FINAL_REPORT_DRAFT.md
